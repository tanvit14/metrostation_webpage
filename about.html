About Pune Metro

History

Pune Metro is a rapid transit system serving the city of Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad in Maharashtra. It was initiated to reduce traffic congestion, promote public transport, and provide safe and efficient travel across the city.

Metro Lines

Pune Metro currently has multiple lines: Purple Line, Aqua Line, Blue Line, and Pink Line. The Purple and Aqua Lines are operational, connecting major hubs in the city and suburbs.

Vision & Mission

Pune Metro aims to provide a modern, eco-friendly, and safe public transport system for citizens. The vision is to reduce traffic congestion, decrease pollution, and provide a convenient travel option for daily commuters.
